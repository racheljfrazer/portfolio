More About Me

I am a Bay Area native that ventured up north to pursue a degree in graphic design. While in college, I realized I also had a love for web development and strived to include those classes into my schedule. Once back in the Bay Area I worked as a developer and designer at a performance marketing start-up. I adored being able expand my multidisciplinary skills while helping create engaging and functional sites for our clients. I was always inquiring so I could better understand the goals of our clients and the roles within our team. Often working independently, I realized that missed collaborating and the sense of community a team could bring you.

When I am not designing, I love traveling to explore new locations. I am also a total foodie. Whether is it cooking up something in the kitchen or hunting down a new restaurant to check out. I use the gym and road biking to work off the food that I consume (mainly cheese, lots of cheese).

Just a little bit about me. If you like what you read, check out my portfolio. If you liked what you see, reach out to me through email.
